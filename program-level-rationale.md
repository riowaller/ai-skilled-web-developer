# Program Revision Rationale: AI-Skilled Web Developer

## Purpose of Revision
The purpose of this program revision is to modernize the existing Web Developer curriculum to reflect a fundamental shift in industry standards. The rapid integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) into web development has changed how web applications are planned, authored, and maintained. 

To ensure our students remain highly competitive and employable, the program is being updated to the **AI-Skilled Web Developer** program. This revision systematically embeds AI-assisted workflows—such as prompt engineering, intelligent code review, and AI-driven architecture analysis—across the core Course Outlines of Record (CORs), while strictly maintaining the foundational coding requirements and academic rigor necessary for CSU transfer and industry success.

## Pedagogical Philosophy: The "Human-in-the-Loop"
This program update operates on a critical distinction: we are not teaching students how to let AI generate websites for them. We are training **AI-Skilled** developers. 

The curriculum emphasizes AI as a force multiplier, requiring students to possess deep foundational logic to direct, verify, and debug AI outputs. The focus shifts from rote syntax memorization to higher-order critical thinking, architecture, and systems evaluation.

## Summary of Impact on Course Outlines of Record (CORs)
The transition to the AI-Skilled Web Developer program requires strategic updates to the existing course sequence (including CIT 82, CIT 85, CIT 28, and CIT 93). Revisions across these CORs will generally include:

* **Updated Course Descriptions & Objectives:** Broadening the scope to explicitly include AI tools, modern workflows, and digital ecosystems. All revised objectives remain measurable and strictly verb-led.
* **Modernized Content Review:** Replacing deprecated technologies with contemporary industry practices (e.g., updating traditional SEO to include AI-driven search discovery).
* **Enhanced Methods of Evaluation:** Incorporating critical analysis assignments where students must evaluate, debug, or justify AI-generated code and business plans, proving mastery over the subject matter rather than reliance on the tool.
* **Text & Course Materials:** Ensuring all zero-cost/Open Educational Resources (OER) and reference materials reflect current capabilities (published within the last 5 years).

## Conclusion
By integrating these AI competencies directly into the existing web development framework, we bridge the gap between traditional web fundamentals and modern, highly efficient industry workflows, ensuring our graduates are distinctly prepared for the current technological landscape.
