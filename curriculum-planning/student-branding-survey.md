# Student Input Survey: Web Development Program Naming

## Form Description
**Title:** Future of the Web Developer Program: Student Branding Survey
**Description:** We are updating our program to ensure it meets the demands of the 2026 job market. Your feedback will directly influence the name of the certificate you earn and how you present your skills to future employers.

---

## Section 1: Settings
* **Collect Email Addresses:** Enabled (to ensure one response per student)
* **Limit to 1 Response:** Enabled
* **Response Editing:** Enabled

## Section 2: Questions

### 1. General Sentiment
**Question Type:** Linear Scale (1 to 5)
**Question:** How important do you believe AI proficiency is for a Web Developer entering the 2026 job market?
* **Label 1:** Not Important
* **Label 5:** Extremely Important

### 2. Branding Preference (Ranking)
**Question Type:** Multiple Choice Grid
**Question:** Please rank the following program titles based on which you believe would be most impressive to a hiring manager on your resume.
* **Rows:**
    * AI-Skilled Web Development
    * Emerging Web Technologies
    * Web Development
* **Columns:**
    * 1st Choice
    * 2nd Choice
    * 3rd Choice
* **Note:** *Enable "Require a response in each row" and "Limit to one response per column."*

### 3. Word Association
**Question Type:** Checkboxes
**Question:** Which of the following words best describes your reaction when you see "AI" in a college program title? (Select all that apply)
* [ ] Modern / Cutting-edge
* [ ] Professional / High-value
* [ ] "Buzzword" / Trendy
* [ ] Intimidating / Difficult
* [ ] Essential / Necessary
* [ ] Overhyped

### 4. Enrollment Driver
**Question Type:** Multiple Choice
**Question:** If two programs had identical content and costs, which title would make you more likely to click the "Enroll" button?
* [ ] AI-Skilled Web Development
* [ ] Web Development (No mention of AI)

### 5. Open Feedback
**Question Type:** Paragraph
**Question:** Do you have any concerns about having "AI" in the official title of your certificate? (For example: concerns about longevity, public perception, or professional reputation).
