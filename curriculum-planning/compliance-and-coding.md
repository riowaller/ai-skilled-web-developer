# 🏛️ Curriculum Compliance & Coding Blueprint (2026)

## 1. CIP Code Alignment (Taxonomy Transition)
Per the December 2026 deadline, we are transitioning from TOP codes to **CIP (Classification of Instructional Programs)** codes. 
* **Proposed CIP Code:** **11.0104 (Informatics / AI Integration)**
* **Rationale:** This code best reflects the hybrid nature of the AI-Skilled program, bridging technical development with AI orchestration.

---

## 2. Common Course Numbering (CCN) & Articulation
A key part of our 2026 strategy is maintaining multiple entry points to maximize enrollment while providing a "Preferred" path for technical rigor.

| Our Course | Advisory Option | CCN / C-ID Alignment |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **CIT 15** | General Entry | BUS 140 / ITIS 120 |
| **CIT 48A** | Technical Entry | ITIS 110 (ICT Fundamentals) |

**Workgroup Consensus:** To maintain enrollment flexibility, CIT 82 will list **CIT 15 OR CIT 48A** as the recommended advisory. This protects the "General Education" pipeline from CIT 15 while signaling that CIT 48A is the preferred path for the "Mechanical Shop" track.

---

## 3. Equity-Focused Assessment (Title 5 Compliance)
Our assessment model is designed to close equity gaps by moving away from monolithic testing in favor of **Performance-Based Mastery**:

* **Verbal Code Reviews:** Students explain their logic in 1-on-1 or recorded sessions. This supports students who may struggle with traditional written exams but possess high technical competency.
* **Supportive Entry (The Bridge):** By using the **"Advisory + Bridge"** model rather than hard Prerequisites, we remove institutional barriers for first-generation and transfer students, allowing them to "skill up" without being blocked by registration hurdles.

---

## 4. Distance Education (DE) Addendum
To remain 100% online/hybrid ready, we must specify the following in our 2026 updates:
* **Generative AI Tools:** Official inclusion of LLMs as "Auxiliary Instructional Materials" for debugging and documentation.
* **Authentication:** Utilizing video-based walkthroughs (Verbal Code Reviews) to ensure academic integrity in the age of AI.
